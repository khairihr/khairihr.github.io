Kuota Pesan: usage-based billing & quota engine

Live in production

Some partners pay Meta through the company's own card, so every billable WhatsApp template send that wasn't metered became money lost. I designed a usage-based billing engine that reads Meta's pricing_analytics, prices each message by category against a self-calibrating rate card, and deducts prepaid credit in real time from the billing webhook, idempotently, with batch-and-retry to stay deadlock-free against live traffic.
